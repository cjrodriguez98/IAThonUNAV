I am Carlos Javier Rodriguez, a dedicated professional with a strong background in Biomedical Engineering. I completed my Master's in Biomedical Engineering at Tecnun Universidad de Navarra in San Sebastián, Spain, where I also pursued my undergraduate studies in the same field. My professional experience includes working as a Computational Biologist at the Bioinformatics Department of Tecnun and as a Full Stack Developer & Data Scientist at XH Digital. I have been recognized for my innovative ideas and research, winning first place for the best Biomedical Idea at the B+INVAS Innovation and Entrepreneurship Program. Beyond my professional and academic pursuits, I am deeply committed to social causes, as evidenced by my involvement in social projects such as the Guatemala Outreach Project.
